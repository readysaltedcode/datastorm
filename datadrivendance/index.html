A data driven dance performance about ocean storm and computer science theory. The theory for this ballet is all about data transmission, security, protocols, internet, networks and weather. This dance performance will include visuals derived from data along side videos projected onto 3D objects to enhance the audience experience. Working with The Met Office and Weather data to influence the choreography and visuals of the ballet.
