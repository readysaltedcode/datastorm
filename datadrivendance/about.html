ABOUT

The second ballet in the series titled "Art of Computer Science" we are looking at internet theory, data transission and weather data to create a beautiful ballet to visualise those abstract concepts and theories. This work is a continuing collaboration between readysaltedcode CIC, industry experts and Battle Abbey School Students.

This project has been funded by Arts Council England and Thomson Reuters, Mobile Technology Division. With support from Google, Intel and SnapperSystems.

The projects continuing aim of reducing fear and enabling interest in the field of computer science. We have already provided successful workshops for young people via the Royal Institutions Summer School Programmes.

Data visualisation were commissioned for the ballet and created by Peter Cook. Projection Mapping Kinect server developed by Alex Shaw from Glastonbridge Software. Choreography by Dr Paul Golz & Camilla Neal of Ephemeris Dance
